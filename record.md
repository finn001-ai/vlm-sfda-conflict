## title
Learning from Conflict Samples in VLM-Guided Source-Free Domain Adaptation

## research question：
Can disagreement between the source-adapted model and VLM be used as a positive signal rather than only treated as noise?

## core claim：
A subset of source/VLM conflict samples is informative under domain shift, and exploiting them with conflict-aware reliability improves SFDA over agreement-only or naive fusion strategies.
